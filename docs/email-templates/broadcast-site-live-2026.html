Six free tools

Soap Calculator (Lye / SAP) →

Enter oils, superfat, water ratio. Get NaOH or KOH amounts plus live hardness, cleansing, conditioning, bubbly, and creamy scores. Plus an 18-recipe library — balanced beginner bars to dish soap, salt bars, milk soap, and natural-pigment formulas.

CNF Readiness Checker →

Paste your ingredients and product details — get a quick readiness report with Hotlist flags, label reminders, and claim-risk notes before you file with Health Canada.

Fragrance Allergen Calculator →

Check which of the 81 EU/Canadian fragrance allergens (Linalool, Limonene, Citral…) cross the disclosure threshold in your formula. Soap, lotion, balm — all product types covered.

INCI Ingredient List Formatter →

Clean up an ingredient list and get suggested INCI ordering plus allergen disclosure reminders.

Cosmetic Cost Calculator →

Calculate cost per batch and per unit including labour, packaging, and overhead — with wholesale and retail price suggestions.

Cosmetic Label Checklist →

Generate a Canadian cosmetic label checklist tailored to your product type, including bilingual reminders and net quantity tips.
